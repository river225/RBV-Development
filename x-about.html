About Us

The Middleman team is dedicated to ensuring you never get scammed. While not a website team, they operate within our Discord server and specialise in facilitating safe, secure trades. A Middleman temporarily holds the buyer’s item during a transaction. The seller then delivers their item to the buyer, and once everything is confirmed, the Middleman releases the buyer’s item to the seller. This process ensures that both parties fulfil their side of the trade, preventing scams.
