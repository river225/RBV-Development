Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

BlockSpin Values ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at https://blockspinvalues.com/

1. Information We Collect

We do not require accounts to use the website. We do not ask you to register, and we do not run a login system for general browsing.

If you contact us by email or through Discord, we receive only what you choose to send (for example an email address or Discord username).

We do not collect payment information; the website is free to use.

2. Browser Storage (localStorage)

We store a small amount of data in your browser on your device using localStorage (not on our servers). Keys include:

  • bsv-theme — your theme choice (for example default or pink) so it can persist when you return

Third-party ad scripts (such as Google AdSense) may set additional storage on your device under their own policies; see Section 3 above.

You can clear this data anytime through your browser’s settings (e.g. “clear site data” for this website).

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, local storage, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to show and measure ads and, where allowed, to personalize ads.

Google’s use of advertising cookies is described in How Google uses cookies in advertising. For more on how Google handles data, see Google’s Privacy Policy. You can manage some ad personalization at Google Ads Settings.

Where required by law (for example in the EEA or UK), personalized or non-essential advertising cookies may only be used with your consent via a consent mechanism we implement or through your browser — follow applicable prompts on the site or use your device/browser controls.

4. Hosting and Technical Information

The site may be hosted on services such as GitHub Pages or comparable hosting. Those providers may automatically log technical information (for example browser type, device type, IP address in server logs, pages requested, and referral URL) for security and operations. We do not directly control those logs; see your host’s privacy documentation.

When you use the site, your browser also sends requests to third parties we rely on (for example Google for Sheets or ads, Discord for invite information). Those services receive standard request data as described in their own policies.

5. How Our Features Use Your Device

Tax calculator and item search: Run in your browser. We do not store your inputs or searches on our servers.

Spreadsheet content: Values and other content are fetched in your browser from public Google Sheets (read-only). Your device contacts Google’s servers to retrieve that data.

Discord: We may call Discord’s public API (for example to show approximate server member counts on banners). That request is made from your browser to Discord. See Discord’s Privacy Policy.

6. Richest Players Leaderboard

Our "Richest Players" feature displays in-game wealth rankings:

  • Official Leaderboard: This is BlockSpin's official richest players leaderboard maintained by the game's community management
  • Data Source: Player usernames, in-game levels and in-game cash amounts are pulled from an official Google Sheet regulated by BlockSpin's Community Lead, Csnsor
  • Roblox Profiles: Clicking a player name links to their public Roblox profile
  • Public Information: All displayed information (usernames, cash amounts) is already publicly visible in-game
  • No Personal Data: We only display Roblox usernames and in-game currency amounts - no real names, emails, or personal information
  • Removal Requests: For leaderboard removal requests, contact BlockSpin's Community Lead Csnsor

7. Third-Party Services and Links

Our website loads data from and links to external services. When you visit or click through, those services’ privacy policies apply:

  • Google Sheets: Item values and other data are read from public Google Sheets in your browser
  • Google AdSense: See Section 3 above
  • Discord: Links to our server; optional API requests for public invite information
  • TikTok: Links to creator profiles
  • Roblox: Links to user profiles
  • ImgBB: Images for items and crew logos

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, and to object to certain processing (for example targeted advertising). Because we minimize what we hold, many requests may be limited to information you sent us directly or to data held by third parties such as Google — use their tools and your browser settings where applicable.

  • Theme and local storage: Clear site data in your browser to remove our localStorage entries
  • Ads: Use Google Ads Settings and your browser’s cookie controls
  • Leaderboard: If your Roblox username appears on the Richest Players list and you wish to be removed, contact BlockSpin’s Community Lead (Csnsor) as described in Section 6 above, or contact us for guidance

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
