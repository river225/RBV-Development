Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

BlockSpin Values ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at https://blockspinvalues.com/

1. Information We Collect

We do not intentionally collect or store any personally identifiable information.

BlockSpin Values is a publicly accessible information website. We do not:

  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Store user preferences or settings
  • Collect payment information (the website is completely free)

2. Automatically Collected Information

Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may automatically collect basic technical information such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referral source (how you found our website)

This information is collected by GitHub Pages for technical and security purposes only. We do not have direct access to this data.

3. Data Storage

No user data is stored. All features on our website operate client-side in your browser:

  • Tax Calculator: Calculations are performed in your browser and not stored
  • Trade Checker: Trade comparisons are performed locally and not saved
  • Search Function: Searches happen in your browser and are not recorded

4. Richest Players Leaderboard

Our "Richest Players" feature displays in-game wealth rankings:

  • Official Leaderboard: This is BlockSpin's official richest players leaderboard maintained by the game's community management
  • Data Source: Player usernames, in-game levels and in-game cash amounts are pulled from an official Google Sheet regulated by BlockSpin's Community Lead, Csnsor
  • Roblox Profiles: Clicking a player name links to their public Roblox profile
  • Public Information: All displayed information (usernames, cash amounts) is already publicly visible in-game
  • No Personal Data: We only display Roblox usernames and in-game currency amounts - no real names, emails, or personal information
  • Removal Requests: For leaderboard removal requests, contact BlockSpin's Community Lead Csnsor

5. Third-Party Services

Our website displays data from and links to external services. When you click these links, you will be subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Sheets: We pull item values and leaderboard data from public Google Sheets (read-only access)
  • Discord: Links to our Discord server (discord.gg)
  • TikTok: Links to creator TikTok profiles
  • Roblox: Links to Roblox user profiles
  • ImgBB: We use ImgBB for hosting item images and crew logos

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

6. Data Security

All information displayed on our website — such as item values, crew logos, and leaderboard rankings — consists entirely of publicly available, game-related data. No private or personal information is stored or transmitted through our site.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
  • You are not tracked across sessions
  • You can use our website completely anonymously
  • If your Roblox username appears on the Richest Players leaderboard and you wish to be removed, contact us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
