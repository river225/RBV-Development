FAQ

Have questions? We've got answers! Click on any question below to expand.

+

We aim to constantly update our values based on market trends and in-game changes such as new content that can affect a items value.

What does "Demand" mean?

+

An items demand refers to how sought after the item is, this is a important factor of items as it can help determine if you will be able to easily sell something. Demand can also add to a items value.

What does "Average Value" mean?

+

Average Value refers to the typical and standard pricing for a item.

What does "Ranged Value" mean?

+

Often placing a Average Value can be difficult for a item as different players sell & buy it for different pricing. Thus Ranged Value tries to cover more ground and is an extra depth to Average Value.

What does "After Tax Value" mean?

+

After Tax Value refers to the additional amount of cash you need to give someone to cover the game's tax. Each time a player drops money, the game takes a small cut as tax.

How do I use the Tax Calculator?

+

Simply enter the amount of cash you want the other person to receive. The Tax Calculator automatically adds the extra amount lost to the game's tax and shows you the total you need to give, ensuring they receive the exact amount you intended.

How much tax does BlockSpin take on trades?

+

Upon death, 72% of your cash is dropped, 8% is taken by the game and 20% is kept and will be on your character upon respawn.

How do I add my crew logo to the website?

+

To suggest your own crew logo for the website, please locate the 'Value Suggestions' channel, Within this channel a tag to suggest crew logos is also offered.
